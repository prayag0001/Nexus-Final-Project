A Little Sumpin' Sumpin'

A WAY SMOOTH AND SILKY IPA

With a certain thing we call a nice wheatly-esque-ish-ness, A Little Sumpin’ Sumpin’ is a truly unique style. Featuring a strong hop finish on a silky body, it’s a hoppy pale wheat ale that is great for IPA fans, but so smooth that hefeweizen fans dig it, too.

STYLE

We’ve watched with some amusement as the internet beer rating sites argues over how to ‘classify’ it. Wheat beer? Sure. Smooth IPA? You got it. Silky wheatly-esque-ish-ness? Definitely. We’re in the camp with Japhy Ryder when he declares to Ray Smith that “Comparisons are odious!”

A Little Sumpin’ Sumpin IPA is 50% wheat, 50% malted Barley, and 100% delicious hoppiness. Hints of bubblegum, pineapple, light cedar & pine. Tons of tropical fruitiness with none of the fruit!

TROPICAL

Little Sumpin’ Sumpin’ utilizes some newer hop varietals that bring all the flavors of a tropical island to your palate. Think POG juice, but with only hops!

SMOOTH & SILKY

Most beer is made primarily with barley malt. We used high proportions of various wheat malts in Little Sumpin’ Sumpin’ to create a smooth MouthFeel that marries perfectly with tropical and floral hop flavors.

Floral

The floral notes from the hops in Little Sumpin’ Sumpin’ compliment the tropical notes to a T. The honeysuckle flavor gives you an impression of sweetness, but doesnt overwhelm the palate. It’s all about balance!
